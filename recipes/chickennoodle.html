Chicken Noodle

Description

This chicken noodle soup has a light, freshly prepared taste.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Chicken broth
  • Mushrooms
  • Jalapenos
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Pasta
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps of Preparation

  1. Chop chicken breast after cooking fully
  2. Chop mushrooms, celery, onions
  3. Boil pasta
  4. Add ingredients into stovetop pot
  5. Heat for 8 minutes
  6. Stir while heating
  7. Serve
