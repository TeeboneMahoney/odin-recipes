Potato Salad

Description

This potato salad is delightfully potato salad, full of potato salad with salad in the form of potatos

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Mayonnaisse
  • Mustard
  • Pickles
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps of Preparation

  1. Boil potatos
  2. Chop potatos
  3. Chop pickles, celery, onions
  4. Add ingredients into a bowl
  5. Mix ingredients together
  6. Refridgerate for 2 hours
  7. Serve
