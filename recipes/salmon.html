Salmon

Description

Sockeye salmon served with carmelized onions, mayonnaisse, and salt and pepper

  • Sockeye salmon filets
  • Mayonnaisse
  • Lemon juice
  • Carmelized onions
  • Johnnies seasoning
  • Onion
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps of Preparation

  1. Slice lines evenly throughout filets
  2. Add salt, pepper and Johnnies
  3. Carmelize onions until ready
  4. Spread mayo on filets
  5. Add carmlized onions
  6. Add lemon juice, salt, pepper etc
  7. Barbecue for 12-16 minutes
  8. Serve
